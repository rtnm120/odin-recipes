Lasagna Recipe

Image sourced from The Spruce Eats

An easy lasagna recipe that is quick to make and satisfying to eat

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 32 ounce jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces of cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°f
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Cook and stir ground beef in the skillet until browned (8-10 minutes approx.), draining grease when browned
  4. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes
  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, 1/4 cup of parmesan, dried parsley, salt, and pepper
  6. Spread 3/4 cup of the cooked sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish, cover with 3 lasagna noodles and 1 3/4 cups of the cheese mixture
  7. Repeat the previous step and top with the remaining noodles, sauce and cheese
  8. Pour 1/2 cup of water along the edges of the dish and cover tightly with aluminium foil. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes
  9. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Once cooked let stand for 10 minutes before serving
