Pumpkin Pie Recipe

An easy pumpkin pie recipe using premade ingredients

Ingredients

  • Store bought pie crust
  • Pumpkin puree
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Eggs
  • Pumpkin pie spice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°f
  2. Mix the puree, condensed milk, eggs and pumpkin pie spice in a large mixing bowl
  3. Pour the mixed filling into the pie crust
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then lower temp to 350°f and cook until the filling has set
