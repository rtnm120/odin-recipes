Sushi Recipe

Image sourced from Homemade Sushi | AllRecipes

Basic crabmeat sushi rolls. The filling can be substituted with any variety of ingredients, the only limit is your imagination

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups water
  • 2/3 cup uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed
  • 1/2 pound imitation crabmeat, flaked
  • 1 avocado peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, peeled and cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons pickled ginger

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300Â°f
  2. Bring water to a boil in a medium pot; stir in rice. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl. Gently stir into cooked rice in the pot and set aside.
  4. Lay nori sheets on a baking sheet and bake in the oven until warm. (1-2 minutes approx.)
  5. Center 1 nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat. Use wet hands to spread a thin layer of rice on top. Arrange 1/4 of the crabmeat, avocado, cucumber, and pickled ginger over rice in a line down the center.
  6. Lift one end of the mat and roll it tightly over filling to make a complete roll. Repeat with remaining ingredients
  7. Use a wet, sharp knife to cut each roll into 4 to 6 slices.
